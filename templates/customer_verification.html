Customer Identity Verification

Online pharmacies must carry out an identity check appropriate for the medicine being supplied.

Accepted Photo ID:

  • 1. Passport
  • 2. Driving License

Note: During a risk assessment, the Pharmacist should gather evidence about the risks for each individual service, medicine and medical device before providing that service.

Our specific identity-checking service uses photo ID verification. There should be a robust process in place.

Common Questions

Can a person choose their medicine before a consultation online?

The digital platform can allow people to give their preferred choice of medicine. However, decisions about treatment are for both the prescriber and the person to consider together during the consultation.
